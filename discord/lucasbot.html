LucasBot


Some time ago, my friend approached me with a request to assist him with a research project he was undertaking for school. After discussing various ideas, we decided to collaborate on developing a straightforward yet functional Discord bot.

This bot, though simple, incorporated a few entertaining and interactive features. When my friend presented this project to his class, it was very well received, and he ended up earning an excellent grade for his efforts!

Features

  • Notification of a new member joining.
  • Respond to greetings like good morning, good afternoon, and good evening.
  • You can give him delicious cookies.
  • A game of 'Rock Paper Scissors'.
  • A game of 'Number under 10'.
  • Issue a warning if someone uses profanity.
  • Display the current number of members.
  • /clearchat command.
